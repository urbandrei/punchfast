About Us

Our Mission Statement

 Our mission is to make meeting your daily fruit goals simple, enjoyable, and accessible. We serve fresh-cut fruit bowls, smoothie bowls, oatmeal bowls, and smoothies that support your health and well-being all in a casual, affordable, and irresistibly delicious way. At The Fruit Stand, were here to nourish our communities, one bowl at a time. 

Fast. Fresh. Fun.

 We never compromise on quality. Our fruits are always fresh, our smoothie bases are constantly evolving, and were always testing new crunchies and drizzles to keep things exciting. Most importantly, we know your time matters so well have your bowl ready in just minutes. 

Freshest Fruit Possible! Never Frozen!

 Our fruits are cut fresh every single day never canned, dried, or frozen. That means every bite is bursting with real flavor and natural sweetness, just the way nature intended. 

Endless combinations!

 With 18 crunchies and 8 drizzles to choose from, there are hundreds of delicious ways to top your bowl making every visit a little different. Craving your go-to combo? Go for it. Feeling bold? Try one of our seasonal crunchies or drizzles to mix things up! 

Our Story

Once upon a time...

 

In the summer of 2020, I was living in the college town of Kent, Ohio, trying to eat healthier and cut out processed foods but I never seemed to have the time. Growing up, I used to love the homemade fruit salads at my grandparents' house. I wanted to bring that simple, fresh feeling back into my life. But between grocery runs and chopping everything up, it felt like a chore.

That was my lightbulb moment.

I figured I couldnt be the only one craving fresh fruit but short on time. So I wrote a business plan, expanded the idea to include smoothie bowls, and designed the menu around a build your own concept fast, fun, and made to order.

From the moment we opened our doors in January 2021, I knew we had something special. Not just an amazing product, but a brand with real purpose.

The Fruit Stand isnt just a business its what I want to do with my life.

Jason Carpinelli
Founder & CEO

Why don't we have online ordering?

We don't have online ordering, because we only want to serve the best quality fruits each and everyday. Some days some fruits don't meet our standards and we won't serve them. Most importantly, we serve a lot of smoothie bowls and we don't want them to melt during the drive home, while they are being delivered to you.
