It All Started in the CLE

Paninis Bar and Grill opened its first restaurant in May of 1986. Since then, Paninis has grown from a single location, late-night beer and sandwich stop to a full-menu, casual dining restaurant with locations throughout Northeast Ohio as well as the Tampa, Florida area. As the franchise continues to expand, were always mindful of our roots in Cleveland. Well always be a Cleveland Company and were proud of that!
