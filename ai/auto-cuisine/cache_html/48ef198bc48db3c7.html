ABOUT TACO BELL AT 805 E Main Street

Find your nearby Taco Bell at 805 E Main Street in Kent, OH. We're serving all your favorite menu items, from classic tacos, burritos, quesadillas and nachos to newer favorites like the Mexican Pizza, Crunchwrap Supreme® and Cheesy Gordita Crunch. Our Cravings Value Menu features great value items like the Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito and the Spicy Potato Soft Taco. Our Cantina Chicken Menu has your daytime cravings covered with the Cantina Chicken Crispy Taco and the Cantina Chicken Bowl. If you're looking for fast food close to the Kent area, stop by Taco Bell today.
