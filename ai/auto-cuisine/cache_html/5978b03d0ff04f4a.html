Welcome to The Battleground

The Battleground Taproom and Mexican Kitchen specializes in traditional Mexican food – street tacos, churros, pozole, tamales, mole, empanadas, and other dishes representative of several regions of Mexico. This is not TexMex – every tortilla is handmade to order, the salsas made fresh daily. We also have a full bar serving a variety of cocktails, house margaritas, and Drink Your Values (DYV) beer. DYV sales support organizations working on local community projects.

Reservations are recommended, but not required!

Where to Find Us

The Battleground is located at 425 Cherry Street in Kent, Ohio. We are just a five mile drive from Tallmadge or Stow, and only eight miles from Hudson and Streetsboro. No matter which direction you’re traveling, The Battleground is easily accessible with free parking in our adjacent lot and on side streets (see our Parking page for available options).
