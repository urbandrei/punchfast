    Eastway Dining

    Door Rate for Eastway Dining Hall:
    Breakfast (Open - 11 a.m.): $9.50
    Lunch (11 a.m. - 4 p.m.): $12.25
    Dinner (4 p.m. - Close): $14.25

    This location is cashless and accepts Credit Cards.

    Proudly served since Spring 2023!

    Just voted one of the 8 Top Indian Restaurants in the US by Wion Desk, Choolaah is a modern take on traditional Indian cuisine. Labeled as authentically unconventional by Choolaah themselves, this fresh take is expanding onto college campuses, with Kent State leading the charge. Try it today in Eastway!

    Eastway Market & Deli

    Eastway Market & Deli, located on the first floor of the Eastway Center, is home to a large selection of grocery items, international brands and Community Corner, which features a variety of products grown and manufactured right here in Ohio. Enjoy exquisite sandwich and wrap creations made with the high quality meats and cheeses from Eastway's Metropolitan Deli.

    This location is cashless and also accepts Meal Exchange, Declining Balance Dollars, and Credit Cards.
